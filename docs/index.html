The Net Project

Welcome to The Net Project.

Title of the intervention The Net-Community Based Malaria Programme
Lead applicant Amici del Mondo - World Friends Onlus (WF)
Partner Department of Health, Kilifi County
Centro Salute Globale della Regione Toscana, A.O.U. Meyer
Country of the intervention Kenya
Location of the intervention Kilifi County (Ganze, Kaloleni Kilifi North, Kilifi South, Rabai)
Duration of the intervention 24 months

The expected output are:

  1. To quantify the number of Malaria suspected and confirmed cases (over 5 and under 5) in 2022, 2023, 2024 and 2025 (first quarter) for communities, dispensaries, Sub-counties and County.

  2. To determine the prevalence of malaria in Children (6-59 months) in 2022, 2023, 2024 and 2025 (first quarter) for communities, dispensaries, Sub-counties and County.

  3. To quantify malaria positive cases (under 5) and total tested for malaria (under 5) in 2022, 2023, 2024 and 2025 (first quarter) for communities, dispensaries, Sub-counties and County.

  4. To quantify the total laboratory test conducted (RDT and microscope), positivity rate and type of infection in 2022, 2023, 2024 and 2025 (first quarter) for communities, dispensaries, Sub-counties and County.

  5. To determine the percentage of the population who slept under a ITN (insecticide treated net) the night before in 2022, 2023, 2024 and 2025 (first quarter) for communities, dispensaries, Sub-counties and County.

  6. To calculate the percentage of population who have access to a ITN (insecticide treated net) the night before in 2022, 2023, 2024 and 2025 (first quarter) for communities, dispensaries (CHU), Sub-counties and County.

vii. To determine percentage of malaria positive cases treated (over 5 and under 5) in 2022, 2023, 2024 and 2025 (first quarter) for communities, dispensaries, Sub-counties and County.

viii. To quantify the number of intermittent preventive treatment of malaria during pregnancy delivered in 2022, 2023, 2024 and 2025 (first quarter) dispensaries (CHU), Sub-counties and County.

ix. To determine the reporting rates of MOH 515 and MOH 705 A/B in 2022, 2023, 2024 and 2025 (first quarter) for community health extension workers and dispensaries included in the project, but also for Sub-Counties and County.
